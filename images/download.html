    Job Name

    Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) has issued a new recruitment notification for the posts of Senior Assistant and Junior Assistant. As per CDAC Pune government job notification, totally 11 vacancies are allotted for these jobs. Eligible aspirants may submit your online application form through CDAC Pune Careers page on or before the last date of 12th March 2019.

    No. of Vacancies:

    Post No. of Vacancies
    Senior Assistant
    04
    Junior Assistant
    07

    Age:

    • Sr. Assistant: Age limit is 35 years.
    • Jr. Assistant: Age limit is 30 years.

    Educational Qualification:

    • Graduate/ equivalent from any recognized university/institution.
    • Check more details of educational qualification in the official website given below.

    Application Fee:

    • General/OBC:- Rs. 500-/
    • SC/ST/EXSM/Female:- Nil

    Selection Process

    • Written Test.
    • Interview.

    Job Location:

    • Pune

    Starting Date for Submission of Online Application Form26.02.2019
